About Me

My name is Victoria I am a groomer from Ukraine. My biggest passion in life is dogs and for more than 10 years I have kept an officially registered dog kennel "VikDen" for Yorkshire Terriers in my hometown Tjernihiv in Ukraine.

The work in the kennel included breeding, grooming and participation in exhibitions. I also educated how to groom the Yorkshire Terrier breed. Many of the dogs from my kennel have received prices in exhibitions held in Ukraine, Europe and America.

I have knowledge in the characteristics of different dog breeds as well as I know the importance of having an individual approach to each animal. Among the services I offer, I do furcuts, bathing, drying, claw trimming as well as daily care.
